Farming

If you see blue arrow pointing at tile, it's someone from your alliance being quicker than you this time. Simply find another tile.

Check how many available troops you have before sending gatherers to tile. If you have less than it's required, find lower level one. Never EVER leave half-empty tile because potato tile doesn't respawn until cleared.

Rallies

Use siege for gathering and include them in troops only if you see that rally is too small for it's target. Avoid sending em to rally. It's slowing things down due to terrible siege's march speed.

Before calling any rally, it's good to announce it on alliance chat, to find out how many people are online and into joining you. This will help you decide how many troops you should send, depending on what type of rally you gonna make. Teamplay mindset required. Few facts:

Lohar

Junior Lohar

  • only 12k tier 1 troops required
  • join rally with smaller amount of troops (let's say 10k), so more different people can participate
  • by doing this, more people will make progress which will positively affects overal progress of alliance

Dauntless Lohar

  • 120k tier 2 troops required
  • you are allowed to join rally with higher amount of troops but still don't forget that letting more people participate is always the best thing
  • use alliance chat to check how many people will join you

Forts

Rewards depends on how much damage you did during the battle. This is a good excuse for sending higher amount of troops. Still, same things could apply from Lohar case. If there is many people around, use chat, communicate, find out how many troops you should send so as many people as possible can participate.
